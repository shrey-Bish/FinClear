# 🎤 SowSmart Pitch Guide - State Farm Hackathon 2026

## 📋 Pitch Structure (5 Minutes)

### 1. The Hook (30 seconds)
**Opening Line:**
> "Raise your hand if you've ever avoided getting insurance because the process felt like homework."

**The Problem:**
- Gen Z is the most underinsured generation
- 64% don't understand basic insurance terms
- Traditional forms take 20+ minutes and feel like a scam
- Zero accessibility considerations

**Transition:**
> "We built SowSmart to fix that. Let me show you."

---

### 2. The Solution Demo (2 minutes)

#### **Landing Page (10s)**
- **Show**: Clean, Lemonade-style design
- **Say**: "This is SowSmart - insurance that actually makes sense. Notice the clean design, friendly language, and single call-to-action. No overwhelming menus."

#### **Meet Nova (15s)**
- **Click**: "Get Started"
- **Show**: Nova's introduction
- **Say**: "Meet Nova, your AI insurance guide. She talks like a friend, not a lawyer."

#### **Quick Questions (45s)**
Type answers quickly:
1. **Name**: "Alex"
2. **Age**: "22"
3. **Living situation**: Select "Renting an apartment"
4. **Income**: Select "$45k"
5. **Biggest worry**: "Expensive medical bills"
6. **Own a car**: "No"
7. **Have pets**: "Yes"

**Say while typing**:
> "Notice how conversational this is. No form fields, just chat. Nova understands context - she won't ask about car insurance if I don't own a car."

#### **Accessibility Features (25s)**
- **Click**: Settings icon (accessibility menu)
- **Show**: Font size controls → increase to XL
- **Toggle**: High contrast mode ON
- **Double-tap**: A message to show simplified explanation

**Say**:
> "Accessibility isn't an afterthought. We have high contrast mode, text resizing, and watch this - double-tap any message to get a simplified explanation of insurance jargon."

#### **AI Recommendation (35s)**
- **Show**: Personalized recommendations page
- **Highlight**: Renters + Health combo suggestion
- **Click**: Voice button (🔊) on "Why this works for you"
- **Show**: It reads aloud
- **Type in chat**: "What's a deductible?"
- **Show**: AI response in plain English

**Say**:
> "Based on Alex's answers, our AI - powered by Google Gemini with RAG on State Farm policies - recommends renters and health insurance. Every section has a voice playback button for accessibility. And you can ask follow-up questions in plain language."

## 🎯 Demo Best Practices

### Before Demo
✅ Clear browser cache  
✅ Close unnecessary tabs  
✅ Full screen browser  
✅ Disable notifications  
✅ Test internet connection  
✅ Have backup video ready  

### During Demo
✅ Speak while you click  
✅ Highlight cursor movements  
✅ Pause for voice playback  
✅ Show, don't just tell  
✅ Keep energy high  

### Handling Questions

**"How accurate are the recommendations?"**
> "We use RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to search actual State Farm policy documents. The AI doesn't hallucinate - it cites specific policy sections."

**"What if users need human support?"**
> "Phase 2 includes escalation to human agents. The chat history gives agents full context, making support faster."

**"How do you handle data privacy?"**
> "All data is encrypted in Supabase. We're GDPR-compliant and only store what's necessary. Users can delete their data anytime."

**"Can this scale?"**
> "Absolutely. Supabase scales automatically. Gemini API handles thousands of requests/second. We're production-ready."
