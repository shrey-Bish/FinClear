# 🔮 SowSmart - Future Scope & Roadmap

## 🎯 Vision Statement

**Transform SowSmart from a hackathon prototype into the #1 insurance platform for Gen Z and Millennials** - making insurance as easy as ordering food, as transparent as Venmo, and as trustworthy as your best friend's recommendation.

## 📅 Development Roadmap

### **Phase 1: MVP Enhancement** (Months 1-3)

#### Claims & Policy Management
- **📸 Photo Claims Submission**
  - Upload damage photos directly in chat
  - AI-powered damage assessment (computer vision)
  - Instant claim status updates
  - Expected payout estimates in real-time

- **📋 Policy Dashboard**
  - View all active policies in one place
  - Visual coverage summaries (no more PDFs!)
  - One-click policy modifications
  - Download digital insurance cards

- **💰 Price Comparison Tool**
  - Compare State Farm vs top 5 competitors
  - Side-by-side feature comparison
  - "Why State Farm is better" AI explainer
  - Price match guarantee checker

#### User Experience
- **🔔 Smart Notifications**
  - SMS/Push for policy renewals
  - Coverage gap alerts ("You just bought a car - need insurance?")
  - Claim status updates
  - Payment reminders

- **🌓 Dark Mode**
  - Full dark theme support
  - Auto-switch based on system preference
  - Accessibility-compliant contrast ratios

- **📱 Progressive Web App (PWA)**
  - Install on home screen
  - Offline access to policy info
  - Push notifications
  - Native app-like experience

#### Backend Infrastructure
- **🔐 Enhanced Security**
  - Two-factor authentication
  - Biometric login (Face ID, Touch ID)
  - Session management
  - Audit logs for all actions

- **📊 Analytics Dashboard**
  - Track user behavior
  - Funnel analysis (where users drop off)
  - A/B testing framework
  - Performance monitoring

**Success Metrics:**
- 10,000 users signed up
- 1,000 policies purchased
- 4.5+ star rating
- <5% bounce rate on onboarding
